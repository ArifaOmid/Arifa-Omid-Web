The Ultimate Prevention

The easiest solution to preventing foodborne antibiotic-resistant disease is the original food safety steps. This is because unlike going organic or becoming vegetarian, it is cheaper and it does not require giving up on eating certain foods. Also, these steps are already supposed to be taught just to prevent sickness in general. By preparing to keep away the smaller diseases, people are also preventing the more major diseases from happening. Sometimes, the original way is the best way.
